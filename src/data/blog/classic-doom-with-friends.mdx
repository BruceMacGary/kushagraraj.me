## Downloading the Game Files
Before you dive in and know how to play, you will need to gather few game files like IWADS and WADS, you will also need zandronum in order to run the game. 
Brief explaination what IWADS, WADS and Zandronum is :-

- IWADS - They are the main game files or the base game, you cant do anything without the iwads.
- WADS - WADS are basically user created mods made for doom due to its flexible source code.
- Zandronum - zandronum is a launcher for doom games since you cant run them on the beast hardware that is the stuff today therefore, you will have to use Zandronnum.

## Installation
### Zandronum 
- After you have successfully downloaded the Zandronum file, install it. It will look something like this.

![image](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/802527252066598943/802543369188278322/unknown.png)

- You may Notice two folder named `IWADS` and `WADS` in this image, dont worry you don't have to download WADS for now. IWADS folder is basically the folder with will contain the IWADS you downloaded using [these links](#iwads).

![iwads](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/802527252066598943/802544439238197258/unknown.png)

- After you have things done till here, you are almost ready to play with your friends after this one step.
 * Navigate to Doomseeker folder > Doomseeker Application.

![Doomseeker](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/802527252066598943/802546798895431701/unknown.png)

![Application](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/802527252066598943/802547513533661184/unknown.png)

- Here, you will have to do some configuration, in order to avoid junk downloads. Go to `Option` > `Configure`.

![configure](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/802527252066598943/802547959144120370/unknown.png)

- Over there, click on File Paths > remove the deflaut by simply selecting them and clicking Remove > Add these Paths (or whereever you choosed to install).

![lol](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/802527252066598943/802548612604624896/unknown.png)

Now you are all setup to join and server hosted by your friends or play with strangers (or people from around the world).

## Bonus
### Playing Singleplayer
If you want to play singleplayer, you can go to the IWADS folder and directly open them.

### Gzdoom

gzdoom allowes you to play doom singleplayer with wad compatibilty, with more video setting options, if you just want to play the vanilla doom, you click on the launcher of gzdoom, then you choose the game you wanna play. For mods like brutal doom, you drag and drop the wad you downloaded into the launcher, and it will pick it up.
