## CBSE Term 1 Exam

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) announced that the date sheet for Term 1 of offline board exams for students of Classes 10 and 12 will be released on Monday, 18 October. Scheduled to be held in November-December 2021, the questions on the exam will follow the objective-type format and will have the duration of 90 minutes each.
